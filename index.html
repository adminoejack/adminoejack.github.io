What OE Jack does

For SREs • DevOps • Platform

OE Jack connects to observability, cloud provider, and ticketing tools to turn alerts into reliable, audited automations with human-in-loop controls.

Event-driven orchestration

Auto-detect incidents from logs, metrics, and traces and attach deterministic runbooks that can be simulated, tested, or auto-executed.

Low-code runbook editor

Drag-and-drop actions, conditional logic, and approval gates — deploy automations without touching YAML or scripts.

Explainable AI & runbook suggestions

AI proposes mitigations and a human-readable explanation. Every suggestion includes data sources, confidence score, and an audit trail.

Cost & config guardrails

Continuously analyze infra usage and propose rightsizing, reserved-savings, and policy fixes with projected ROI estimates.

Integrations & audit trail

First-class connectors (Prometheus, Datadog, Sentry, PagerDuty, Slack, AWS, GCP, Azure, Jira) and immutable audit logs for compliance.

Shadow & test runs

Runbooks can be shadowed against production traffic to validate effects and surface unintended consequences before activation.

FAQ

How does OE Jack integrate with my monitoring stack?
We provide secure connectors and webhooks for Prometheus, Datadog, NewRelic, Sentry and can ingest alerts from any webhook-capable tool. Integrations are configured per-team and support scoped permissions.
Is automation safe for production?
Every runbook can be configured with approval gates, shadow mode, and simulated dry-runs. Audit logs and immutable evidence are attached to every action for compliance.
Can I host OE Jack in my VPC?
Yes. Enterprise plans support private VPC deployments, dedicated networking, and on-premise connectors for sensitive environments.
